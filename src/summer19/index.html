What about the academic part?

There is a good reason people call our planet ‘the blue planet’, 70% of it is covered with water! Could it be that using this huge area of oceans as a big power plant is the ultimate way to get rid of polluting fossil fuels or nuclear power plants? Come aboard and join us in the beautiful Ghent to explore the possibilities of blue energy through a series of interesting classes and labs
