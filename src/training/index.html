Developing students, the mission of BEST

To fulfil this mission we offer students all kinds of training sessions, both on soft skills and hard skills. Take a look below to find out what you can do!

Training in BEST

We believe soft skills are very important competences in life. To develop our members to the fullest, we offer soft skills trainings to them to improve their communication, project management, leadership, time management, conflict management, … One by one skills which are useful both in your BEST work as in later life!

We will be working in partnership with Möbius on the topic of data storytelling in other words creating clear and compelling stories with data. As an engineer, you are an expert in analysing and interpreting data, but communicating your findings to a non-technical audience can be a challenge. In this workshop, you'll learn how to turn your data into compelling stories that resonate with your audience. By utilizing resources like ChatGPT, you can speed up the creative process of producing your data story.
