What about the academic part?

Artificial Intelligence will exceed humans in reasoning and intelligence. Did this sentence terrify you? Or did it trigger your interest? If it was the latter, then Ghent is the place to be this summer! Join us in a series of classes and practical sessions, where we will explore all the fascinating things AI can do.

Starting from the basic concepts of machine learning, we will introduce neural networks and deep learning. We will then move on to convolutional neural networks for image-related tasks, and finish with an application to arcade games, using image-based reinforcement learning. You’ll never get bored, cause we are using several different ways of learning. After these 10 days in Ghent, you will definitely want more!
