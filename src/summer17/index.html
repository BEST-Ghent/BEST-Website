Going on a course with BEST Ghent guarantees an unforgettable experience.

About our course

You will be staying in the vibrant city of Ghent, surrounded by other enthusiastic students that are internationally minded. Ghent University is a top 100 university and is celebrating it’s 200th year of existence this year. You can celebrate with us, this summer. There will be inspiring classes, unforgettable moments full of happiness and joy, new friendships and the most epic social activities. Get ready for the ultimate BEST experience!

Let's create some expectations!

BEST Courses take place all over Europe and are organised by a particular Local BEST Group in cooperation with their local university and last between one and two weeks.

Classes

Learn more about 3D printing through interactive lectures, workshops and company visits in cooperation with Ghent University.

City

Discover Ghent and the Ghentian culture under guidance of true Ghentian students.

Fun

Participate in lots of social activities. A weekend trip with hiking and canoeing, themed parties every evening, and much more...

Friendships

Make friends from all over Europe during one of the most intense weeks of your life.

Experience

Enrich your CV with international experience in order to distinct yourself from other students and attract companies.

Cost-effective

You will pay max €45 for the entire course, including meals, lodging and other social activities.

What about the academic part?

Do you have a limitless imagination? Do you want to do things that seem impossible to make? Have you ever wondered how the magic of 3D printing works? Or do you want to master the art of it? If the answer to those questions is yes, then Ghent is the place to be this summer!

You will learn all about the 3D printing process, and you will apply this knowledge when you 3D print your own BEST souvenir. Find out about the ultra-modern fab lab where you will be taught and where you will work yourself. A very motivated team of teachers is waiting to teach you, but there’s more: you will also get to chance to learn from some leading companies in the 3D printing industry. After these 10 days in Ghent, you will definitely want more! You’ll never get bored, cause we are using several different ways of learning.
