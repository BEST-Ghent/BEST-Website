Design the future. Today.

The sense of innovation, combining creativity with technicality and efficient teamwork. During EBEC you can put all of these skills to the test and lead your own team to victory, or at least to an evening full of fun and challenges.

The concept of EBEC.

EBEC consists of 88 local rounds, 15 regional or national. round. The winning team of each round gets prizes and a ticket for the next round. Maybe your team will even make it to the EBEC Benelux!

Meet the Team Design.

Together with a friend, you will have to use basic materials (wood, springs, magnets, nails...) and tools (glue gun, saw, drill…) to build a prototype that satisfies a given problem as best as possible. Points are awarded for the ingenuity, durability and performance of your prototype. Some examples of challenges are ‘build a rocket which can go in the air up to 25 m’ or ‘make a M&M shooter’.
Do you have a creative yet practical mindset that can solve problems like no other? Then don’t hesitate to challenge yourself and test your engineering skills!

How wil this be organized?

You form a team of 2 and gather together at one of your places. The material you need will be distributed and recollected before and after the competition. To stimulate your creative brain, we will provide a delicious PrEBEC survival package.

During the competition, you will be gathered in a zoom meeting in which you can stay in contact with the organisers, Unilin, the jury and other teams while working on your design.

Time schedule

18u45:     You join the zoom meeting. We’ll test if your settings work.
19u00:     Short presentation of our partner Unilin
19u15:     Presentation of the challenge
19u30:     Working on the challenge. You will be able to ask questions in the zoom meeting.
21u40:     End of the challenge. A short video in which you test your solution should be submitted.
21u45:     Presentation of the videos to the jury
23u00:     Announcement of the winners
