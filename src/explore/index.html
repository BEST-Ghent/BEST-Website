Explore BEST

You probably know BEST Ghent from Summer Courses, EBEC or the ‘Internationale Shotjesgoliarde’. But is this all BEST and its active members do during an entire year?

Three facts:

  • Last year, with the help of BEST, our members travelled more than 50 times to events all around Europe. They got the opportunity to improve their soft & hard skills and broadend their social networks with multinational companies & students from al over Europe.
  • This year, BEST Ghent organises 4 events for technology students from all over Europe to come to Ghent.
  • After this year, we will have organised more than 50 internal events, with the goal of developping students' soft skill, improving education all over Europe and improving our own organisation.
