Come to Sustainability Trends: Master Carbon, Circularity & Compliance

Are you ready to explore the key trends shaping the world of sustainability? Join us gain hands-on experience tackling real-world challenges. During this workshop, you'll dive into the most important sustainability trends— Carbon, Circularity, and Compliance — and apply what you've learned by working on a practical business case study. After the session, stick around for a reception with free croques, snacks, and drinks to wrap up the evening in a relaxed atmosphere!



The event takes place on Wednesday, October 23, 2024 in Ghent. You are expected at 19h30 at the GreenHub (Sint-Pietersnieuwstraat 33, 9000 Ghent). Don’t miss this opportunity to deepen your sustainability knowledge and put it into action!
